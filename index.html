Little About Tigray

Found in the Eastern highlands Africa, Tigray is steeped in rich history and cultural heritage, often celebrated as a land of origins. This area is not only known for its stunning landscapes but also as the cradle of the Axumite Kingdom, one of the most powerful civilizations in ancient Africa which was named one of the biggest civilizations alongside with Egypt, Roma, and Persia. Axum was not only a center of trade but also a melting pot of cultures. It was one of the first states in sub-Saharan Africa to mint its own coins and adopt Christianity as the state religion around 350 CE, making it a pivotal player in early Christian history12. The influence of Axum extended to various regions, facilitating trade routes that connected it with distant lands like India and Rome. This cultural exchange enriched its society, leading to advancements in art, architecture, and governance.
